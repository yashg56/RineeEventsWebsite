Who We Are

There are immeasurable benefits of hiring an event planner. We want the process of planning and executing your event to be just as memorable and happy as the event itself.That is why we have a creative and flexible approach to all our planning, making something as fun as it can be.

SENIOR EVENT DESIGNER

Born and raised in San Diego, Hallie absolutely loves planning and designing events. Her strength in customer service and her passion for design make her our most important event stylist. She has built many great relationships with vendors over her career, and is constantly pushing herself to do bigger and better things, using her colleagues as motivation.

SENIOR EVENT PLANNER

Born and raised in Southern California, Sarah is the best when it comes to planning and executing the events. With her extensive knowledge of San Diego, she can make any event a possibility, and her passion for organization ensures that every event goes smoothly. She loves weddings, and her favorite part of her job is helping out on the day of the wedding.
